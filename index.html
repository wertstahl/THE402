The Story of 402 loops t0.3

In 2006, after finishing the first Wertstahl album "://firewall" and with the next album "Kontrol" under construction, i jokingly mailed to DeDokter, "well let us do some sort of ping pong game - you do one loop, i do one loop and so forth. In some kind of communications game. Rules are simple: no longer than 10 seconds, content completely free, except it should be industrial-related."

Quickly we had quite a few loops ready and really enjoyed the process of just working on twisting and bending sound, without the burden of having to care about an album context.

It was not much later when we somehow decided to do 200 loops, just for the fun of it. And when the 200 loops were done, we decided "Why not do 200 more - just because we can?".

And as we were nearing the 400 mark, we thought "Okay, and what are we gonna do with them?".

It took us from 2008 to 2022 to decide and even find a solution for the decision.
That is why this is here.

If you like 'em, feel free to share or download. But please - if you use them, send us a short email who you are and where you used our work.

And ofcourse, the awesome Gapless5 library by Rego Sen is at your disposal on Github.
Enjoy!
